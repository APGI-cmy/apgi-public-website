## Purpose

This addendum aligns APW architecture with the root website intent canon.

---

## Architectural principle

The APW information architecture must be journey-led.

The public narrative is:

`loss opportunity -> maturity assessment -> gap identification -> maturity roadmap -> implementation support -> monitoring and assurance -> training and coaching -> resilient operations`

---

## Homepage requirement

The homepage architecture must support sections for:

- APGI's holistic loss-prevention proposition;
- malicious and non-malicious loss;
- organisational maturity and opportunity generation;
- free maturity assessment entry point;
- APGI ecosystem as a connected maturity journey;
- services, training, and platform handoffs.

---

## Platform/APGI Hub requirement

The Platform/APGI Hub page must not be a plain module-card grid.

It must support:

1. a journey introduction;
2. maturity assessment and roadmap explanation;
3. ecosystem role mapping;
4. module cards or sections placed inside the journey;
5. free assessment and ISMS/APGI Hub handoff CTA;
6. polished pending destination states where live module links are not yet available.

---

## Free assessment requirement

The architecture must provide a safe public entry point for the free maturity assessment.

APW introduces the assessment, explains the maturity indication, and hands the visitor to the approved ISMS/APGI Hub destination for deeper platform functionality.

---

## Case-study and interaction requirements

The architecture should allow future anonymised case studies and layered education using hover/click/ask/train where appropriate.

Any hover/click/ask/train pattern must have mobile and keyboard equivalents. Hover-only disclosure is not acceptable.
