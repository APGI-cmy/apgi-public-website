## Non-Delegable Evidence Rule

The builder may not close a requirement by saying it is intended, planned, obvious, or implied. Stage 12 must contain observable evidence or an explicitly dispositioned exception.

---

## Appointment Limit

This obligation map appoints the builder and defines obligations only. Implementation belongs to Stage 12.
