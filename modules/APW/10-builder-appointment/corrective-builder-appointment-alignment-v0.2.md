## 1. Corrective Appointment Finding

The previous builder appointment must not be reused for the upgraded build without binding the builder to App Description v0.2 and the corrective pre-build criteria.

---

## 2. Required Appointment Conditions

A future upgraded-build appointment must require the builder to acknowledge:

1. PR #11 was a functional scaffold but failed design-quality expectations.
2. App Description v0.2 is the authoritative product definition.
3. Corrective QA-to-Red v0.2 must be green before final acceptance.
4. Multi-page IA is mandatory.
5. Services, platform/app, and training page depth are mandatory.
6. Professional visual design quality is mandatory.
7. Public-only/static-first scope remains mandatory unless CS2 changes it.
8. Vercel/browser evidence is mandatory.

---

## 3. Green Standard

Builder appointment is green only when the appointed builder is bound to corrected product/design/evidence obligations and cannot treat a deployable scaffold as complete.
