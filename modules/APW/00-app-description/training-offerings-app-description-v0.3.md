## Purpose

This addendum expands the APW training requirement from a narrow VPSHR/course-placeholder presentation into a substantive APGI training catalogue presentation.

The website must present APGI training as a core public offering, not only a Thinkific button.

---

## Classroom / Contact Delivery Catalogue

The public Training page must present the following classroom/contact-delivery offerings from the APGI training catalogue:

1. Security Investigations - 4.5 days.
2. Security Supervisor - 4.5 days.
3. Diamond Mining and Processing - A Security Perspective (DMP) - 5 days.
4. Security Surveillance and Control Monitoring (SSCM) - 4.5 days.
5. X-ray Scanning and Search Techniques - 3.5 days.
6. Security Risk Management Training Programme - 4.5 days.
7. Voluntary Principles on Security and Human Rights - Level 1 - 3.5 days; e-learning pathway also available.
8. Voluntary Principles on Security and Human Rights - Level 2 - 3.5 days.
9. Voluntary Principles on Security and Human Rights - Level 3 - 1.5 days.
10. Security Project Management - 5 days.
11. Customer Relations in Security - 2.5 days.

All listed classroom/contact offerings must provide a contact CTA rather than implying online self-enrolment when no e-learning link exists.

---

## E-Learning Platform Surface

The Training page must also show the public e-learning/platform surface separately, including:

- Voluntary Principles on Security and Human Rights Level 0.
- Voluntary Principles on Security and Human Rights Level 1 / Refresher 2025.
- Mastering Diamond Control Standards: A Comprehensive Guide.
- Tiger Kidnapping Awareness Programme.
- Tiger Kidnapping: Know the Threat / Assess Your Risk.

E-learning offerings must link through the approved public Thinkific storefront unless a course-specific public URL is supplied later.

---

## Success Criteria

The APW Training page is green only when:

- classroom/contact offerings are visible as a proper catalogue;
- e-learning offerings are separated from classroom/contact delivery;
- VPSHR is presented as one family of training, not the whole training business;
- each offering has audience, delivery, duration, and CTA behavior;
- no Thinkific admin URL or private training management surface is exposed;
- all unavailable/private details use honest placeholder or contact states.
