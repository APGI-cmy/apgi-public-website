## 1. Corrective Checklist Finding

---

## 2. Required Builder Obligations

The upgraded builder checklist must require the builder to:

1. load App Description v0.2 and all corrective alignment artifacts;
2. build every required public page;
3. implement the premium design system;
4. build services, platform/app, and training pages with meaningful depth;
5. preserve public-only/static-first scope;
6. produce browser evidence for all pages and viewports;
7. prove every corrective QA-to-Red item green;
8. stop if the result trends back toward a thin scaffold.

---

## 3. Green Standard

Builder checklist is green only when a future builder can execute the upgraded site without inventing product/design requirements during implementation.
