## Purpose

This addendum adds builder checklist obligations tied to the root website intent canon.

---

## Builder checklist additions

Before handover, the builder must confirm:

- `APW_WEBSITE_INTENT.md` was loaded.
- The implemented site presents APGI as a holistic loss-prevention and maturity-improvement partner.
- Malicious and non-malicious loss are explained in accessible language.
- The maturity journey is visible and understandable.
- The free maturity assessment entry point exists and routes or hands off toward ISMS/APGI Hub.
- The Platform/APGI Hub page explains the ecosystem as a connected maturity journey, not only a set of app cards.
- MMM, Risk Management, PIT, Incident & Intelligence, RADAM/Data Analytics/Remote Assurance, Skills Development/Training, and ISMS/APGI Hub are mapped to maturity progression.
- Any hover/click/ask/train interaction is accessible without hover-only dependency.
- Case-study content is anonymised unless permission is recorded.
- APW does not add unapproved backend/platform runtime scope.

---

## Builder handover evidence

The builder handover must cite implemented locations for:

1. loss-prevention proposition;
2. malicious and non-malicious loss explanation;
3. maturity journey narrative;
4. free maturity assessment CTA and handoff;
5. Platform/APGI Hub journey explanation;
6. ecosystem role mapping;
7. training and consultation handoffs.

If evidence is missing, Foreman/QP should treat the handover as incomplete.
