## 1. Purpose

This artifact defines the evidence a future builder must prepare to collect during the later build and Stage 12 evidence phases.

---

## 2. Evidence Checklist

| Evidence ID | Evidence Area | Required Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| BE-001 | Routes | Browser evidence that all required public pages render. |
| BE-002 | Navigation | Header, footer, mobile menu, and CTA link evidence. |
| BE-003 | Homepage | Hero, previews, positioning, CTAs, and visual treatment evidence. |
| BE-004 | Services | Services content and CTA evidence. |
| BE-005 | Platform | Module cards, status, link or pending state evidence. |
| BE-006 | Training | Training offering cards and Thinkific handoff evidence. |
| BE-007 | Contact and legal | Contact links, Privacy, and Terms evidence. |
| BE-008 | Design system | Palette, typography, cards, sections, and responsive visual evidence. |
| BE-009 | Accessibility and mobile | Keyboard, focus, mobile, and reduced-motion notes. |
| BE-010 | Metadata | Page title, description, canonical, and social metadata inspection. |
| BE-011 | Validation | Validation command output. |
| BE-012 | Deployment | Vercel URL, build logs, output directory, and commit SHA. |
| BE-013 | Scope boundary | Source/validation confirmation that the public-site boundary is preserved. |
| BE-014 | QA map | Evidence mapped back to Stage 6 QA IDs. |

---

## 3. Evidence Rule

A future build may not be called green until evidence is mapped to Stage 6 QA IDs and reviewed under Stage 12.
