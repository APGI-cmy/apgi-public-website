## Purpose

This addendum adds root-intent risk to the APW IAA pre-brief posture.

---

## Assurance focus added

IAA review for APW must check whether the prebuild and build artifacts preserve the APGI website intent.

Key assurance questions:

1. Does the work cite or clearly incorporate `APW_WEBSITE_INTENT.md`?
2. Does it preserve APGI's holistic loss-prevention proposition?
3. Does it explain malicious and non-malicious loss?
4. Does it present organisational maturity as the route to reducing opportunity for loss?
5. Does it provide or preserve the free maturity assessment entry point and ISMS/APGI Hub handoff?
6. Does it prevent the Platform/APGI Hub page from becoming a flat app directory?
7. Does it explain each ecosystem module as part of the maturity journey?
8. Does it preserve training, coaching, remote assurance, and implementation tracking as support mechanisms?
9. Does it protect case-study confidentiality?
10. Does it avoid adding unapproved backend or platform runtime scope to APW?

---

## Assurance risk

The primary strategic assurance risk is that a technically working and visually polished APW site could still fail by losing APGI's philosophy.

IAA should treat this as a substantive failure mode, not an editorial preference.
