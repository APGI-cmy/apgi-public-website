## Functional Requirements

| ID | Requirement |
|---|---|
| APW-FR-TRN-031 | The Training page shall present a classroom/contact-delivery course catalogue. |
| APW-FR-TRN-032 | The Training page shall present e-learning/platform offerings separately from classroom/contact offerings. |
| APW-FR-TRN-033 | Each classroom/contact course card shall show title, audience, delivery mode, classroom duration, and contact CTA. |
| APW-FR-TRN-034 | Each e-learning card shall show title, platform availability, lesson/time indicator where known, and Thinkific storefront CTA. |
| APW-FR-TRN-035 | VPSHR Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3 classroom offerings shall be represented as a training family. |
| APW-FR-TRN-036 | Diamond Mining and Processing / Diamond Control training shall be represented as both classroom/contact and e-learning where applicable. |
| APW-FR-TRN-037 | Tiger Kidnapping offerings shall be represented as e-learning/platform offerings. |
| APW-FR-TRN-038 | The page shall not expose Thinkific administration links or imply online enrolment where only contact delivery is available. |
| APW-FR-TRN-039 | The page shall provide a group/custom/classroom training contact path. |
| APW-FR-TRN-040 | Training content shall remain public-safe and avoid unapproved client-specific claims. |

---

## Negative Requirements

- Do not create a training backend.
- Do not add LMS administration.
- Do not add learner accounts.
- Do not add payment processing inside APW.
- Do not expose course-specific links unless supplied as public links.
